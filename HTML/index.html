GOOD ENOUGH

IS NEVER ENOUGH.

Our engineered surfaces go beyond the competition with a patented, innovative fusion process that bonds the floor’s wear layer to our revolutionary PLAETech Foundation. Unlike most other systems, we don’t just put some adhesive between two layers and hope it sticks. Our process is executed so that you benefit from layers that are permanently bonded and will not move or shift – resulting in durability, versatility and longlasting performance that cannot be rivaled today.
